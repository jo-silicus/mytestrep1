Reports
This option will take you to the screen where you will be presented with the options for viewing the Reports.
Individual Summary Report: It will show the user's row score and stanine score corresponding to various activities.
General Individual Report: It will outline the individual’s overall management and processing abilities i.e the information content, memory, management approach, clarity of purpose, decision making and directing , leadership functions, verstality of the user.
ExecMap Leadership A Report: It will outline the individual’s leadership abilities in the Quad X format.
ExecMap Leadership E Report: It will outline the individual’s leadership abilities in the Alpha Leadership format.
Assessment Summary Report: It will outline the details of session,start date, activities of all the users of a particular group.
