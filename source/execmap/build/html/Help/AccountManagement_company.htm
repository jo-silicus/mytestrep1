Account Management-Company

Select a company: Click on the option button beside the company name to select that company.
View the groups comprising a company: Clicking on View Groups will take you to the screen where the groups comprising that particular company are displayed.
Add a new company: Clicking on the 'Add' Button will bring up the screen through which a new corporate account will be created.
Edit an existing company's profile: Clicking on the 'Edit' button will bring up the screen through which an existing (selected) company account's data may be edited or changed.
View an existing company's profile: Clicking on the company will bring up the screen through which an existing (selected) company's profile, as stored in the databases, is displayed.
Delete Disabled Companies: This button should be pressed in order to delete the disabled cpmpanies. After clicking this button, you can choose to delete all the disabled companies or can choose to delete the selected company only.
Enable/Disable a company: Click on the 'Enable/Disable' button to enable or disable a currently selected company. If the selected company is enabled ,then it will become disabled anf if the selected company is disabled , then it will become enabled.
