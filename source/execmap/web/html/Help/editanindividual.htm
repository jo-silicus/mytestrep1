Edit A Company User
On this page you will be presented with a number of fields where you may change the information pertaining to the existing company user.
Some of the fields are required, that is they must be filled in before proceeding further. Such fields have yellow background

This is information is filled into two sections, viz.

  1. User's Information
  2. Personal Information

Each of these sections are explained below.
User's Information
Password (Required): In this field, you may change the password that will be assigned to the company user.
Re-type Password (Required): Here you must retype the password that you enter in order to confirm it.
Reminder Phrase : Here the reminder phrase may be filled
Personal Information
First Name, Last Name (Required): In this field , you can change the name of the company user.
Email (Required):Here the email address of the company user can be edited.
Status(Required): In this field you can change the status of the company user as 'Enabled' or 'Disabled'.
Notes : Here you can change all the user specific information.

At the bottom of the screen are two buttons. The Submit button should be pressed to actually enter the information into the database and register the company user.

The Reset button can be presses to clear all the fields and start over.
