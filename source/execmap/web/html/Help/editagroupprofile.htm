Edit A Group
This page presents a number of editable fields whereby you may make changes to the profile of the group as it exists in the database. This page is divided into the following sections: 
Some of the fields are required, that is they must be filled in before proceeding further. Such fields have yellow background

This is information is filled into three sections, viz.

  1. Group's Information
  2. Group Manager 's Information

Each of these sections are explained below.
Group's Information

Group Information: Here you may change any information that is generally pertinent to the group being referred to.

Group Manager's Information
 
All the fields in this section are required. They pertain to information about the Group Manager who will be managing the various users that comprise the company's test takers.
First Name,Last Name (Required): In this field the name of the group manager ,you wish to assign to this group may be edited.
Password (Required): Here the password that will initially be assigned to the group manager may be edited.
Retype Password (Required): Here you must re-type the password that you filled in to confirm it.
Reminder Phrase : Here the reminder phrase may be filled
Email (Required): Here the email address where the company can be contacted may be edited.
Send Email: After pressing this button, a conformation mail will be sent to the registered email of the group manager.

At the bottom of the screen are two buttons. The Submit button should be pressed to actually enter the information into the database and register the group.

The Reset button can be presses to clear all the fields and start over.
