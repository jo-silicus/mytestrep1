Notice for New Customers

Intellicue's online tools use the most advanced Web technologies available today.
Intellicue uses the latest programming languages to ensure that our tools are optimized for use over the Internet. This allows Intellicue to provide you with complex Internet applications that cannot be accomplished with older technologies. The use of this advanced technology has a drawback - Intellicue's tools push the performance limits of older computer hardware.

Intellicue designed its products to be ahead of the technology curve - where high speed internet access, such as DSL, is available for everyone. Your Internet connection speed will directly impact the performance of Intellicue's online services.

We encourage all potential customers to read our FAQs before signing up for our services.
Currently, Intellicue's products are only compatible with clients running Windows operating systems.
Full Macintosh support will be added in Summer 2000.

Please note that if you are using a dialup connection or a congested ISP to access the Internet, you will experience some delays as Intellicue's applications transfer data to your computer. Intellicue's systems require at minimum, a 56k modem connection. However, Intellicue recommends that its users have an even faster connection, such as a cable modem or DSL. Intellicue makes no warranties for the level of performance of its products over slow or congested internet connections.
