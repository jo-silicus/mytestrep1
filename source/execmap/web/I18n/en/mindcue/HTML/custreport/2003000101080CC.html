Processing Traits

All of us deal with information differently. Some of us are fascinated by detail while others can't be bothered and are more inclined to take the big picture approach when thought is required. In general, you already know how you handle information in everyday life. You are probably satisfied with the progress you have already , and the successes you will accomplish.

Still, by looking at various combinations of your assessment scores, there might be some fascinating new insights into how you process information. If any are revealed through your assessment, they will be listed here.
