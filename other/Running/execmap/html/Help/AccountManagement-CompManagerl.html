Account Management

This page deals with the the various groups of users (that have been registered through your company) you will be managing as a Company Manager/Group Manager. Here you can perform the following actions:

Select a company: Click on the option button beside the company name to select that company.
View Groups comprising a company: Clicking on View Groups will take you to the screen where the groups comprising that particular company are displayed.
Edit an existing company's profile: Clicking on the 'Edit' button will bring up the screen through which an existing (selected) company account's data may be edited or changed.
View an existing company's profile: Clicking on the company will bring up the screen through which an existing (selected) company's profile, as stored in the databases, is displayed.
