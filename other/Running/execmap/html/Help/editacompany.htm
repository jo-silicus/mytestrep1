Edit A Company

In this page you can edit the information about the company by making changes to the desired fields. For storing changes in the database, certain fields having yellow background must have a valid value.

This is information is filled into three sections, viz.

  1. Company's Information
  2. Company Manager 's Information
  3. Customize

Each of these sections are explained below.
Company's Information
Company Name (Required): Here you can change name of the company you have registered already.
Address, Zip, City, State, Country (Required): Here you can change the address of the company registered with you.
Email (Required): Here the email address where the company can be contacted can be changed.
Company Information: Here you may edit other pertinent information about the company that you feel is not covered by the fields.
Send Email: After pressing this button, a conformation mail will be sent to the registered email of the company manager.
Company Manager's Information
First Name, Last Name (Required): Here the name of the company manager can be edited.
Password (Required): Here the password that will initially be assigned to the company manager can be edited.
Retype Password (Required): Here you must re-type the password that you filled in to confirm it.
Reminder Phrase:Here the reminder phrase eneterd by the company manager can be edited
Customize

In this section you may change the information that is needed for customization of the online tests in context of the CoBranding strategy of the system. The fields in this section again refer to the particulars of the company, and to CoBranding options and are primarily self-explanatory

At the bottom of the screen are two buttons. The Submit button should be pressed to actually enter the information into the databases and register the company.

The Reset button can be presses to clear all the fields and start over.
