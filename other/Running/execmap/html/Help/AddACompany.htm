Add A Company
Through this page you can add a new company. This requires filling up the information about the company to be filled into the database. For this you must fill in certain fields which are described below.
Some of the fields are required, that is they must be filled in before proceeding further. Such fields have yellow background

This is information is filled into three sections, viz.

  1. Company's Information
  2. Company Manager 's Information
  3. Customize

Each of these sections are explained below.
Company's Information
Company Name (Required): Here you must type the name of the company you want to register.
Address, Zip, City, State, Country (Required): Here you must type in the address of the company.
Email (Required): Here the email address where the company can be contacted must be filled.
Company Information: Here you may fill other pertinent information about the company that you feel is not covered by the fields provided.
Company Manager's Information
First Name, Last Name (Required): Here the name of the company manager must be filled in.
User Id (Required): Here the User Id of the Company Manager, as will be used be the assigned company manager, must be filled in.
Password (Required): Here the password that will initially be assigned to the company manager must be filled in.
Retype Password (Required): Here you must re-type the password that you filled in to confirm it.
Reminder Phrase : Here the reminder phrase must be filled
Customize

In this section you may fill in the information that is needed for customization of the online tests in context of the Cobranding strategy of system.The fields in this section again refer to the particulars of the company, and to Cobranding options and are primarily self-explanatory.

At the bottom of the screen are two buttons. The Submit button should be pressed to actually enter the information into the databases and register the company.

The Reset button can be presses to clear all the fields and start over.
