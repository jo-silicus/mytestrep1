College Comparison

 

Although there are many factors that go into predicting success in a college situation, it might prove valuable to show your results against the average results shown by graduating college students. Of course these scores will vary because of the differences in major fields of study, but they have been averaged together so you can compare your score with someone who has successfully made it through college. They do not guarantee your success or should even be used as a prediction of success. They are simply offered as a way of comparing your score against a benchmark.
