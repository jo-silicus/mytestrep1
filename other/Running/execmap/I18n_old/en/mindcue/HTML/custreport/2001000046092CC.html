Learning Traits

 

Combining the scores from several different activities that were presented during the entire assessment, and viewing them as a whole, can indicate traits you will exhibit during school or other learning opportunities. Remember, the Structure of Intellect model does not include a focus on behavior. It acknowledges that environment, motivation, and leadership can all play a very definite part in a your learning ability.
