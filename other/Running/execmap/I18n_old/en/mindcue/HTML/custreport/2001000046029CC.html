Your assessment shows that you are underdeveloped in the ability to comprehend material presented in semantic terms. If you have been having problems in school, this is most likely the cause: most material taught in schools is taught in a semantic fashion. Long dissertations and lengthy prose only serve to confuse you. A a more abbreviated fashion of explaining concepts is probably where you learn best. Look at your symbolic scores. If you have good symbolic ability, then an outline form or approach will be more effective. Nonetheless, a fair semantic ability will be required throughout the rest of your life. So you should try to improve this ability by reading for pleasure. Start with clear, short books , but then go on to more lengthy and involved material.
