Problem-Solving

Problem solving deals with your ability to collect information, correctly relate the various pieces of information, and converge upon an answer. In strict SOI terms, this was called convergent production. It is a measure of your ability to logically come up with the solution to the problem you are facing. In school work, it entails the ability to take the facts that one has memorized, bring them together in the correct process, and come up with the appropriate solution.
