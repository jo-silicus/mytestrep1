Use Intellicue Here

What follows is Intellicue's uniquely incisive assessment. You'll be asked for your age and most recent level of formal education. The tool will then run off approximately 15-24 game-like activities, each lasting anywhere from 45 seconds to 5 minutes. Proceed at your own pace, but the assessment usually takes about 70 minutes. It can be approached in different sessions, so long as it is completed overall within two weeks. Just sign off when you want a break: when you login again, the assessment will take you right back to where you left off. As soon as you're done, the results are automatically analyzed and detailed reports are ready for downloading or printing. You can even analyze the results against various jobs and career patterns contained in the accompanying database.
